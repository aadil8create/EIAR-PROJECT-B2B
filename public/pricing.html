Frequently Asked Questions

What are lead credits?

Lead credits are consumed when you receive a matched enquiry. Each credit allows you to view one buyer enquiry that matches your subscription preferences.

Can I upgrade my plan?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes will be reflected in your next billing cycle.

Do unused credits roll over?

Monthly plan credits reset each month. Pay-per-lead credits never expire and roll over indefinitely.

Is there a contract?

No contracts required! You can cancel your monthly subscription at any time with no cancellation fees.
